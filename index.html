So, You're Addicted To Skitta.

Skitta is a highly addictive and dangerous narcotic of unknown origin. It comes in the form of a yellowish, pus-coloured e-liquid which is vaporised and inhaled. It has a distinct chlorine smell with organic undertones, “like public swimming baths”.

It was first identified in April 2225 on Earthmoon, but quickly surged throughout the Jovian system, particularly around the L4 Trojans.

Skitta makes you feel certain. Confident. In control. Users report a sense of flow, invincibility, and absolute clarity... the feeling that nothing can touch you and nothing can go wrong.

This is not what is happening.

In practice, motor control degrades. Hands shake. Grip strength fades. Movements become jittery and weak. Thought patterns narrow. Behaviour grows compulsive, neurotic, and paranoid, even as the user feels more assured than ever.

Withdrawal from Skitta is highly inconsistent. Effects vary day-to-day, person-to-person, and unpredictably. Some lose sight. Some lose skin. Some go mad.

If a lapse in judgement led you to try Skitta, the following information has been collated to facilitate your recovery. Take it one day at a time.

░░░ Skitta Addiction ░░░

▉ ▉ SPECIAL CONDITION ▉ ▉

When you take Skitta, you gain the following effects, in chronological order, after your high.

▉ FIENDISH:
At your next Long Rest, make 1 CON Save [DC 10] per Dose. On a failure, record 1× stack of Skitta Addiction.

▉ CRASHED:
After any Long Rest, roll Xd6 (= Stacks), and experience each Contagion (spell) table result until your next dose or your next Long Rest. Take 1d4 Poison damage for every duplicate result rolled.

▉ RESET:
After a Clean (no Skitta) day, make a WIS Save [DC 15] to remove 1× stack of Skitta Addiction.
